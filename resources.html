Resources

We've curated and simplified a comprehensive list of resources for all the offerings at both the Pratt Brooklyn and Pratt Manhattan campuses. Our goal is to help you easily navigate the wide range of programs, services, and opportunities available at each campus. We hope you'll discover something new and make the most of everything Pratt has to offer, whether you're exploring academic resources, student support services, or grabbing a snack!
